Mission • Vision • Human-first network

Mission & Vision

We’re building a human-verified social network where people — not bots, click-farms, spam engines, or rage-bait machines — set the tone.

Our Mission

Make social spaces trustworthy again by ensuring every account is a real human through World ID. With identity handled privately, Hash Humanity can focus on what matters: conversation, creation, and community.

Who We Are

Builders and pragmatists. We ship quickly, keep the product focused, and prioritize clarity, reliability, and user trust. Hash Humanity is intentionally minimal — a clean, stable space for verified people.

Why We’re Doing This

Open social got wrecked by bots, spam, and incentives that reward outrage. Verification at the door helps remove the bot problem without turning the product into another ad-soaked machine.

The Bigger Picture

We’re laying the groundwork for a network where creators get paid directly through Pulse tipping, language barriers are reduced with built-in translation, and data extraction is not the business model.

No dark patterns. No fake metrics. Just people talking — and tipping — in the open.

How It Works

1) Verify with World ID — Prove you’re human without sharing biometric data with Hash Humanity.
2) Join the feed — Post, translate, react, and connect.
3) Tip with Pulse — Send WLD micro-tips through World App or WalletConnect.

What’s Next

Device-first onboarding, better internationalization, creator tools, stronger voice-room experiences, and export paths so users are not trapped inside the platform.
