    Mission · Vision · Human-first network

    Mission & vision.

    We're building a human-verified social network where people — not bots, click-farms, spam engines, or rage-bait machines — set the tone.

    01 — Mission

    Make social trustworthy again

    Every account is a real human, verified through World ID. With identity handled privately, Hash Humanity can focus on what matters: conversation, creation, and community.

    02 — Who we are

    Builders and pragmatists

    We ship quickly, keep the product focused, and prioritize clarity, reliability, and user trust. Hash Humanity is intentionally minimal — a clean, stable space for verified people.

    03 — Why we're doing this

    Open social got wrecked

    Bots, spam, and incentives that reward outrage broke the open internet. Verification at the door removes the bot problem without turning the product into another ad-soaked machine.

    04 — The bigger picture

    A network that pays people, not itself

    Creators get paid directly through Pulse tipping, language barriers drop with built-in translation, and data extraction is not the business model.

    No dark patterns. No fake metrics. Just people talking — and tipping — in the open.

    You are the proof, not the product.

    05 — How it works

    Three steps to get in

    1 Verify with World ID. Prove you're human without sharing biometric data with Hash Humanity.
    2 Join the feed. Post, translate, react, and connect with verified people.
    3 Tip with Pulse. Send WLD micro-tips through World App or WalletConnect.
    06 — What's next

    Where we're headed

    Device-first onboarding, deeper internationalization, creator tools, stronger voice-room experiences, and export paths so people are never trapped inside the platform.
